About Me

I am a Junior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison triple majoring in Computer Sciences, Economics, and Mathematics. I am also pursuing the certificate of Art, Studio and focus in Graphic Design. I plan to graduate in 2021 Fall (December 2021).

I am interested in the applications of Computer Sciences, especially data analysis and machine learning, to other fields to try to solve challenging problems. I applied the analysis of acoustic data into the research of the marine ecosystem. I am also exploring the interdisciplinary between Computer Sciences and Economics.

From January 2020, I have the opportunity to utilize Machine Learning in the research on the rainforest biodiversity for the project “Tropical Stethoscope” in the Sound Forest Lab with professor Zuzana Burivalova, collaborating with professor Claudia Solis Lemus and professor Daniel Pimentel Alarcon.

Starting in 2021 Spring, I will work towards my CS honor thesis advised by professor Christos Tzamos and focus on Mechanism Design.
